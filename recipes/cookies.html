Cake Mix Cookies

Description

Cake mix cookies with just three simple ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix together cake mix, eggs and oil in a large bowl.
  2. Make little balls with the dough and set on ungreased cookie sheets.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit (175 degrees celcius) for 4-10 minutes.
